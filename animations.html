Animations

In this course I learned animation skills using Adobe after effects, Photoshop and AI. Inspired by street photography and electronic music I made a series of animations. My goal was to make ambient style videos which are in sync with the music.

strange doorway

In this animation, I combined my own photographs with abstract shapes and AI-generated imagery. My goal was to create a visual journey that pulls the viewer into the image, where the scenes gradually become stranger and more abstract, blurring the boundary between reality and imagination.

music: Tachyon by Yaleesa Hall & Malin Genie

Who's home?

In this animation, I started with a stock image and adjusted its colors to set the right mood. I then individually turned the windows purple in Photoshop and synced them so they are flickering to the music. As a result, the entire building seems to communicate with the rhythm of the sound.

music: Pemble Alan by Konduku

peeking bricks

In this animation, I set out to create a skyline that continually expands and reshapes itself. By layering different images, I wanted to evoke the feeling that the skyline is watching you, rather than the other way around.

music: Peel me easy by Karenn
