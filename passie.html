interactive digital exposition about the rise of house music in the Netherlands

A digital exposition exploring the rise of house music in the Netherlands. In this project, I created an interactive interface that brings together multiple media elements, including music, fragments of a documentary, and animated typography. The viewer can explore and interact with these elements, resulting in a layered and immersive experience that captures the raw energy of the early club scene and the atmosphere of that era.
