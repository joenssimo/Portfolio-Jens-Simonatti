parody on movie poster from "The exorcist"

In this course I made a parody on the legendary 1974, The Exorcist movie poster. I learned communication by exploring Semiotics. By Using Gestalt principles, I established visual hierarchy, balance, and flow, guiding the viewer’s eye. Finally, with Rhetoric, I persuaded through visuals, evoking emotion, building credibility, and communicating a clear message. By combining these elements, I created designs that are not only visually appealing but also impactful and memorable.
